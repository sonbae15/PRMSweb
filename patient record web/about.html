Patient Record Management System

Patient Records Management System is a system that streamlines and automates the process of saving the patients' personal and medical information to the healhcare's database. The system is also developed to digitalize the management of the patients records in the healthcare institution. The Patient Records Management System comes with a desktop and an Android application to be used by the patients, their parents, or their guardian. The application can be downloaded here in the Patient Records Management System's website and you can download it just clicking the Download App to be directed to the download page.
